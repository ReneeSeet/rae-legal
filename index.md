# Privacy Policy for Rae

_Last updated: April 22, 2026_

Rae ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices when using the Rae mobile application (the "App").

## 1. Information We Collect

### a) Account Information
When you create or use an account, we may collect:
- Email address
- Basic account identifiers (such as user ID)

### b) User Content
When you use Rae features, we may collect:
- Journal/reflection text you submit in the App
- Generated outputs (such as mood summaries, quotes, reminders, and related results)

### c) Device and Usage Data
We may collect limited analytics and technical information, such as:
- App events (for example, feature usage and error events)
- Device/app metadata needed for performance and reliability

### d) Permissions Data
If you grant permission, we may access:
- Microphone input (for voice reflection/speech features)
- Notification permissions (to send reminders)

## 2. How We Use Information
We use information to provide app functionality, generate outputs, authenticate users, improve reliability, and send reminders you opt into.

## 3. Third-Party Services
- Supabase (auth/storage)
- OpenAI (text processing for generated outputs)
- Firebase Analytics (usage analytics, where enabled)
